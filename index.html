Symbolistics

Symbolistics is a group dedicated to exploring a range of disciplines, including formal sciences (like mathematics and logic), natural languages, and casually philosophy and natural sciences. We investigate both these individual areas and the interconnectedness between them. We use tools like pen and paper, Mathematica, GeoGebra, Haskell, Agda, Lean, Lisp (Clojure/Racket), large language models (LLMs), and other AI methods in our investigations. We also develop tools based on these resources.
